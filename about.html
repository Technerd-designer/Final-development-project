Atlas library is one of the biggest prides of our community.In 2016,members of the community came together and formed an organization,mostly comprising of teachers, parents and our local leaders.This organization was formed after we realized that our own young people lacked access to quality reading material.We then realized that part of the problem was the poor state of the library, which not only had few books available, but those available were outdated and torn.


Seeing as upgrading the education standards of our children was one of our top priorities,we came together and came up with a concept for a new,state of the art library. With contributions from our members,three years later,in 2019, this concept became the full blown reality that we see today!


Fully equipped and top tier,it offers reading material in all departments and therefore all subjects.Throught it,we aim to ensure that our students and teachers are fully catered for by having adequate reading material.In addition to that, we also have story books for the children and novels for the reading fanatics who love to indulge in books during their free time!


So logon to our system today,take a look and discover,read and borrow all types of books! Happy reading!
